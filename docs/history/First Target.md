# **First Target: Cold Boot Sequence**

**Project:** Stellar  
**Sequence Type:** Opening Tutorial / Narrative Initiation

## **1\. The Blackout Phase**

The experience begins in total darkness. The player hears the rhythmic, mechanical thrum of a failing life-support system. Green BIOS code flickers across the neural interface:

* BIOS CHECK... OK  
* OXYGEN LEVELS... 18% (CRITICAL)  
* NEURAL LINK... ESTABLISHED

## **2\. The First View**

The HUD flickers to life, revealing the interior of a cracked **Tier 1 Scavenger Pod** cockpit. The pod is drifting through a "Graveyard" of shattered starships, backlit by the haunting glow of a distant blue sun. Debris and massive metallic hulks drift silently past the window.

## **3\. AI Awakening**

The onboard AI, a glitchy precursor to the **Overseer**, delivers the primary objective:  
"Pilot, the Reactor Drone has been lost. We are drifting on residual battery. To survive, we must secure high-density matter for the Fabrication Unit. Deployment of the last Expendable unit is authorized."

## **4\. Mission Objective: The Dart Maneuver**

The player is tasked with their first survival action: targeting a nearby **Level 1 Silicate Asteroid**. Without permanent assets, the player must utilize their only resource: a single **Expendable "Dart" Drone**.

### **Gameplay Steps:**

1. **Scanning:** Manually lock onto a "High-Iron Trace" within the asteroid field.  
2. **Launching:** Trigger the Dart Drone, which impacts the asteroid at high velocity.  
3. **The Impact:** The kinetic force shatters the rock into manageable chunks.  
4. **Manual Retrieval:** Pilot the Scavenger Pod to collect the floating debris using the gravity scoop.

*Conclusion:* Upon securing the material, the **Level 5 Steel Plating** recipe is unlocked, marking the transition from scavenger to fabricator.

## **5. Left Pane Mission Navigation Guidance**

During the first-target navigation steps, the UI now shows a persistent guidance overlay in the lower-right of the **left pane**.

1. **Manufacture Step Active:** Overlay points to **Fabrication Lab**.
2. **Repair Step Active:** Overlay points to **Repair & Retrofit**.
3. **Menu Glow:** The corresponding left-menu button still pulses with the `NEXT` badge.
4. **Dismissal Behavior:** Dismissing the overlay hides it for the current step and character, and it reappears when the mission advances to the next guided step.
